# CSE422_Adult_Income_Classification

In today's economy, a person's earning is frequently influenced by personal and professional
factors. In order to go beyond common statistics to understand the practical factors that
contribute to financial success, this study dives deeply into the ‘UCI Adult Income’ dataset. Our
aim is to determine whether an individual makes more than $50,000 annually by developing and
evaluating multiple classification models such as KNN, NN. These insights offer an
understanding into how demographics, such as occupation and education affect financial status.
